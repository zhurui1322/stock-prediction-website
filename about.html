    About The Project

    What Is Our Project?

    Rutgers Stock Predict and Simulator provides innovative price-prediction technology for active Day Traders, Short- and Long-term Investors.We develop advanced web-based software for stock market forecasting and analysis and at the same time we provide stock simulator serve for our registered user.

    Our high-quality solutions are based on research done by our team members.For each details in this project ,we spend lots of time and effort, and we are committed to delivering the most accurate predictions for financial professionals, active traders, and investors.

    What Makes Us Different?

    The accurate algorithm- In our project we researched many different algorithms and compared their perfromance in the prediction. Our software mathematically compares existing stock-trading patterns with previous patterns and analyzes these interconnections. By using our project you can make money in both short term and long term.

    Application- We do not only provide web serve, we also provide cellphone serve, our clients enable get the stock information whenever and wherever by using our android application.

    Stock simulator-This is our star feature, as for our registered user, we will provide a simulator system for them. in this system, the clients can buy or sell the phantom stock, and analysis the stock market quotations. This will help the new people who set foot in the stock.
